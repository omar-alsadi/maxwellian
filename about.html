Maxwellian has assembled a visionary core team with over 100 years of combined business and technology experience. Our CEO started more than 20 years ago with an insatiable desire to make clean, fiber optical tech accessible.

His focus was to combine the effectiveness and longevity of optics with affordable materials. It is how Maxwellian was born, and our commitment to cost-effective, hearty sensing technology resonates more poignantly every day.
